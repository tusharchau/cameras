  ABOUT CAMERA'S


 

  A camera is an optical instrument that captures
  a visual image. At a basic level, cameras consist
  of sealed boxes (the camera body), with a small 
  hole (the aperture) that allows light through to
  capture an image on a light-sensitive surface 
  (usually a digital sensor or photographic film).
